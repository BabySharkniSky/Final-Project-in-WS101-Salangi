FAQs
What is your booking policy?
We require a 500 non-refundable deposit to reserve your preferred date. The remaining balance and security deposit must be settled upon check-in, either in cash or via accepted payment method.
Can I modify or cancel my booking?
You may move your booking date up to 3 times before it is forfeited--just message us at least 3 days before your booked date. . Cancellations are subject to the non-refundable policy.
What payment options are available?
We accept Cash, GCash, and Bank transfers.
Check-in/ Check-out Time
Check in is 2PM and check out is 11AM the following day.
Is there Wi-Fi at the resort?
Yes, free Wi-Fi is available in all common areas.
Do you allow pets?
Yes, pets are allowed, but they are not allowed in the pool and bedding.
